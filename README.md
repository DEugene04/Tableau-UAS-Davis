# Key Insights
- In 2018, China and India has the most population
- There is a positive linear correlation between year and Indonesia's population
- In 2018, America placed 1st in terms of the world's GDP
- The graph shows GDP growth with an increasing trend. The relationship between GDP and year is a **positive linear relationship**. In other words, as time goes by, GDP also increases. However, we can also observe several significant declines in GDP in **1998** and **2015**.
- There is a positive linear trend between population and GDP
